For GHG Reporters

In response to the FY2008 Consolidated Appropriations Act (PDF) (613 pp, 1.5M) (H.R. 2764; Public Law 110-161), EPA issued the Greenhouse Gas Reporting Rule (74 FR 56260) which requires reporting of greenhouse gas (GHG) data and other relevant information from large sources and suppliers in the United States.

The For GHG Reporters section provides information and resources for businesses and institutions that are or may be subject to reporting under the Greenhouse Gas Reporting Program (GHGRP). The pages in this section provide more detailed GHGRP information.

Topic Description
Proposed Rules Currently active regulatory proposals related to the GHGRP.
Resources by Subpart Guidance and supporting materials arranged by industry.
Training and Testing Opportunities Current training opportunities and archived training presentations.
Rulemaking Notices Federal Register notices and links to more information.
e-GGRT Data Reporting System Information and updates to the online system used to report emissions to the GHGRP.
Confidential Business Information Information and resources regarding the handling of confidential business information reported under the GHGRP.
